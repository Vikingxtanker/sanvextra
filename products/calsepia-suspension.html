CALSEPIA™ Suspension

Generic name: Calcium, Phosphorus, Magnesium, Zinc & Vitamin D3 Suspension (Sugar Free | Liquid Chocolate Flavor)

Storage: Store in a cool and dark place. Protect from light. Do not freeze. Shake well before use. Keep out of reach of children.

Product overview

CALSEPIA™ Suspension is a balanced mineral and vitamin supplement formulated to support strong bones, teeth, and overall skeletal health. It provides calcium and phosphorus in an optimal ratio, along with magnesium, zinc, and vitamin D3, which together enhance bone mineralization, improve calcium absorption, and help prevent nutritional deficiencies.

The sugar-free liquid chocolate formulation ensures excellent palatability and compliance, making it ideal for children, elderly patients, pregnant and lactating women, and individuals who have difficulty swallowing tablets.

Salt composition (per 10 ml / two teaspoonful)

  • Calcium Phosphate IP — Equivalent to Elemental Calcium: 300 mg
  • Calcium Phosphate IP — Equivalent to Elemental Phosphorus: 150 mg
  • Magnesium Hydroxide IP — Equivalent to Elemental Magnesium: 75 mg
  • Zinc Gluconate USP — Equivalent to Elemental Zinc: 4 mg
  • Vitamin D3 IP — 400 IU
  • Flavored non-syrupy base q.s. (Appropriate overage of vitamin added to compensate for loss on storage.)

Indications / Uses

  • Calcium and phosphorus deficiency
  • Weak bones and brittle teeth
  • Osteoporosis and osteopenia
  • Bone health during pregnancy and lactation
  • Growth and development in children
  • Fracture healing and recovery
  • Muscle cramps and fatigue due to mineral deficiency

Benefits

In Bone Strength: Calcium and phosphorus are essential for the formation and maintenance of strong bones and teeth.

In Calcium Absorption: Vitamin D3 enhances intestinal absorption of calcium and phosphorus, ensuring better utilization.

In Muscle & Nerve Function: Magnesium supports normal muscle contraction, nerve transmission, and reduces muscle cramps.

In Growth & Immunity: Zinc plays an important role in bone growth, tissue repair, and immune function.

Regular use helps maintain optimal bone density and prevents deficiency-related complications.

How it works

CALSEPIA™ Suspension works by supplying essential minerals and vitamins required for healthy bone metabolism:

  • Calcium & Phosphorus help in bone formation and strength
  • Vitamin D3 improves absorption of calcium and phosphorus
  • Magnesium supports muscle and nerve function
  • Zinc aids bone growth and tissue repair

Together, they help maintain strong bones and prevent mineral deficiencies.

Dosage & administration

Take this medicine in the dose and duration as directed by the physician. Shake well before use. Use a measuring cup or spoon for accurate dosing. May be taken with or after food.

Do not exceed the recommended dose unless advised by your doctor.

Side effects

CALSEPIA™ Suspension is generally well tolerated. Some individuals may experience mild side effects.

  • Constipation
  • Bloating
  • Nausea
  • Flatulence

These side effects are usually mild and temporary. Consult your doctor if they persist.

Precautions & safety advice

  • Shake well: Always shake well before use for uniform dosing.
  • Do not freeze: Protect from freezing temperatures.
  • Storage: Store in a cool and dark place and protect from light.
  • Keep out of reach: Keep out of reach of children.

Storage & packaging
