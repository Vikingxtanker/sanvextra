ASERRO™-SP Tablet

Generic name: Aceclofenac (100 mg) + Paracetamol (325 mg) + Serratiopeptidase (15 mg)

Storage: Store below 30°C. Protect from moisture and heat.

Product overview

ASERRO™-SP Tablet is a fixed-dose combination formulated to provide multimodal relief from pain, inflammation and swelling associated with musculoskeletal conditions and postoperative pain. The combination pairs an NSAID (Aceclofenac) with Paracetamol (an analgesic/antipyretic) and Serratiopeptidase (a proteolytic enzyme) to address pain, inflammation and inflammatory exudates together.

Indications / Uses

  • Short-term relief of pain and inflammation in musculoskeletal and joint disorders
  • Symptomatic management of osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis and ankylosing spondylitis
  • Post-operative pain and inflammation

Benefits

The combination delivers multimodal analgesia: Aceclofenac reduces inflammation and pain via inhibition of prostaglandin synthesis; Paracetamol provides additional central analgesic and antipyretic effects; Serratiopeptidase helps reduce local swelling and may speed recovery by breaking down inflammatory exudates. Taken together they can provide faster and more complete symptomatic relief than single-agent therapy in appropriate patients.

How it works

Aceclofenac (NSAID) reduces synthesis of prostaglandins, lowering inflammation and pain. Paracetamol works centrally to reduce pain and fever. Serratiopeptidase is a proteolytic enzyme that breaks down abnormal protein deposits and inflammatory exudates at the inflamed site, facilitating resolution of swelling.

Dosage & administration

Use ASERRO™-SP exactly as prescribed by your healthcare professional. Do not chew, crush or break the tablet — swallow whole with water. It is recommended to take it with or after food (or milk) to reduce gastrointestinal discomfort. Follow the prescribed duration — prolonged or unnecessary use increases the risk of adverse effects.

Side effects

Most side effects are mild and transient. Contact your physician if symptoms persist or worsen.

  • Nausea, vomiting
  • Stomach pain, indigestion, heartburn
  • Loss of appetite
  • Diarrhea
  • Dizziness, drowsiness
  • Increased liver enzymes

Precautions & safety advice

  • Alcohol: Avoid alcohol while taking this medicine — it may increase gastrointestinal irritation and liver risk.
  • Pregnancy: Safety during pregnancy has not been established. ASERRO™-SP is not recommended in pregnancy unless clearly needed and prescribed by a doctor.
  • Breastfeeding: Use is not recommended while breastfeeding — potential for harm to the nursing infant.
  • Driving / machinery: May cause dizziness or drowsiness. Avoid driving or operating machinery if affected.
  • Allergies: Do not use if you have known hypersensitivity to Aceclofenac, Paracetamol, Serratiopeptidase, or other NSAIDs.

Monitoring & important information

For prolonged therapy your physician may monitor renal and liver function tests and full blood counts. Long-term NSAID use can increase the risk of gastrointestinal bleeding, renal impairment and other serious adverse effects. Report any unusual bleeding, black/tarry stools, severe abdominal pain, jaundice, reduced urine output or persistent gastrointestinal symptoms immediately.

Storage & packaging

Storage: Store below 30°C. Protect from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of children.

Packaging: Pack size and carton artwork may vary — refer to the product label supplied with the pack.
