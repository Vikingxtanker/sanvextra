FEZEST™ Suspension

Generic name: Liposomal Iron (Ferric Diphosphate) with Folic Acid & Vitamin B12 (Sugar Free | Tamarind Flavour)

Storage: Store in a dry place below 30°C. Protect from moisture and direct sunlight. Shake well before each use. Keep out of reach of children.

Product overview

FEZEST™ Suspension is a liposomal iron-based health supplement enriched with folic acid and vitamin B12, formulated to support healthy haemoglobin levels and red blood cell (RBC) formation.

The liposomal iron technology enhances iron absorption while being gentle on the stomach, reducing common side effects such as gastric irritation, constipation, and metallic taste. Its sugar-free tamarind flavour ensures excellent palatability and better compliance.

Indications / Uses

  • Iron deficiency
  • Iron deficiency anemia
  • Low haemoglobin levels
  • Nutritional anemia
  • Increased iron requirement in women
  • Fatigue and weakness due to iron deficiency
  • Support during menstruation

Benefits

In Iron Deficiency & Anemia: Liposomal iron helps restore iron stores efficiently and supports haemoglobin synthesis.

Better Absorption & Gut Friendly: Advanced liposomal delivery improves bioavailability and minimizes gastrointestinal discomfort.

In RBC Formation: Folic acid and vitamin B12 support healthy red blood cell production and maturation.

Improved Energy Levels: Helps reduce tiredness, weakness, and fatigue associated with low iron levels.

Regular intake as recommended helps maintain optimal iron status.

How it works

FEZEST™ Suspension works by providing essential nutrients required for blood health:

  • Liposomal Iron supports haemoglobin formation and oxygen transport
  • Folic Acid helps in RBC production
  • Vitamin B12 aids cell maturation and helps prevent megaloblastic anemia

The liposomal formulation ensures enhanced absorption with minimal gastric irritation.

Dosage & administration

Recommended usage: Take 15 ml once daily or as directed by your healthcare professional.

  • Shake well before use
  • Use a measuring cup or spoon for accurate dosing
  • Do not exceed the recommended daily usage

Side effects

FEZEST™ Suspension is generally well tolerated.

Possible side effects:

  • Mild nausea
  • Abdominal discomfort (rare)

These effects are uncommon due to the liposomal formulation.

  • Should not be used as a substitute for a varied diet
  • Consult your physician if you are taking any medication before consuming this product
