DOMVEX-PLUS™ Capsules

Generic name: Esomeprazole 40 mg (Enteric Coated) + Domperidone 10 mg (Immediate Release) + Domperidone 20 mg (Sustained Release)

Storage: Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Store below 30°C. Protect from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of children.

Product overview

DOMVEX-PLUS™ Capsule is a combination medicine used to treat gastro-esophageal reflux disease (GERD), acidity, heartburn, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, and gastric motility disorders. It combines a proton pump inhibitor (Esomeprazole) with a prokinetic agent (Domperidone) to provide effective and long-lasting relief from acid reflux and associated symptoms.

Esomeprazole reduces excess acid production in the stomach, while Domperidone improves gastrointestinal movement and prevents nausea and vomiting. This dual action makes DOMVEX-PLUS particularly effective in patients with acid reflux accompanied by bloating, fullness, or delayed gastric emptying.

Indications / Uses

  • Gastro-esophageal reflux disease (GERD)
  • Acid reflux and heartburn
  • Peptic ulcer disease
  • Indigestion (dyspepsia)
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Bloating and abdominal discomfort due to delayed gastric emptying

Benefits

In Acid Reflux & GERD: DOMVEX-PLUS Capsule helps relieve acidity, heartburn, and regurgitation by reducing excess stomach acid and preventing acid from flowing back into the food pipe.

In Nausea & Vomiting: Domperidone enhances gastric motility and blocks nausea-causing signals, helping to control vomiting and a feeling of sickness.

In Indigestion & Bloating: By improving stomach emptying, DOMVEX-PLUS reduces bloating, early satiety, and abdominal discomfort.

For best results, take the medicine regularly as advised by your doctor. Do not exceed the recommended dose or duration of treatment.

How it works

DOMVEX-PLUS Capsule is a combination of two medicines:

  • Esomeprazole is a proton pump inhibitor (PPI) that works by blocking the acid-producing pumps in the stomach, thereby reducing acid secretion.
  • Domperidone is a prokinetic and anti-emetic agent that improves stomach and intestinal movement and prevents nausea and vomiting.

Together, they provide effective relief from acid-related and motility-related gastrointestinal disorders.

Dosage & administration

Take DOMVEX-PLUS Capsule exactly as prescribed by your doctor. It is usually taken before meals for maximum benefit. Swallow the capsule whole with water. Do not chew, crush, or open the capsule. Do not stop the medicine without consulting your physician.

It is usually taken before food, preferably in the morning.

Side effects

Most side effects are mild and usually go away as your body adjusts to the medicine. Consult your doctor if they persist or worsen.

  • Headache
  • Dry mouth
  • Abdominal pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Constipation
  • Flatulence
  • Dizziness

Long-term use should be under medical supervision.

Precautions & safety advice

  • Alcohol: Alcohol can increase stomach acid and reduce the effectiveness of this medicine.
  • Pregnancy: DOMVEX-PLUS should be used during pregnancy only if clearly needed and prescribed by a doctor.
  • Breast-feeding: Domperidone may pass into breast milk. Use only under medical supervision.
  • Driving: May cause dizziness or drowsiness. Avoid driving if you feel unwell.

Storage & packaging

Storage: Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Store below 30°C. Protect from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of children.
